Transform Your Beauty Parlour Operations

Beauty Track is a smart customer management system for beauty parlours and salons that helps track customers, manage services, and improve daily efficiency.

About This Project

A simple and efficient customer management system built for beauty parlours to track customers, manage services, and improve daily operations.

✤ Easy Customer Entry

Staff log in and quickly add customer details such as name, contact, service, time, and billing.

✤ Daily Auto Reset

The staff dashboard refreshes every day at midnight, showing a clean view while securely saving all past data.

✤ Full Admin Control

Admins can view complete records, manage staff and services, track repeat customers, and access analytics.

✤ Smart Business Insights

View reports, monitor revenue, identify popular services, and make better business decisions using accurate data.

Staff Portal

Give your team the tools they need to efficiently manage daily customer visits with an intuitive interface designed for speed and simplicity.

✤ Quick Customer Entry

Easily add customer details, services, time, and payment.

✤ Daily Dashboard

See only today’s customers with an automatic midnight reset.

✤ Secure Login

Individual login access for each staff member.

✤ Complete Visit Records

Track services, timings, repeat visits, and payments.

✤ Auto Refresh

Daily data resets while all records remain safely stored.

Admin Portal

Gain full oversight of your salon operations with powerful analytics, staff management, and comprehensive reporting tools.

✤ Analytics Dashboard

View service-wise visitors, daily and weekly stats, and total billing.

✤ Staff Management

Add, manage, and control staff accounts with secure access.

✤ Service Setup

Create and manage service types for staff selection.

✤ Visitor History

Access complete customer records with service and billing details.

✤ Recurring Customer Alerts

Track repeat customers and get reminder notifications.

✤ Revenue Tracking

Monitor total earnings and overall financial performance.
